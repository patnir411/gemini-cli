üí° Architecture Highlights

  • Multi-Layer Design: Clear separation of concerns across 6 distinct layers
  • Event-Driven: AsyncGenerators and streaming throughout
  • Resilient: Retry logic, fallbacks, and error recovery at every level
  • Extensible: Plugin architecture via MCP, extensions, and custom tools
  • Observable: Full telemetry with OpenTelemetry integration
  • Secure: Policy engine, sandbox execution, and folder trust
